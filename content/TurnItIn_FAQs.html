Q: Can I resubmit?
A: Yes - you can resubmit at any time before the deadline. Your original submission is overwritten by later submissions.

Q: How do I submit documents with appendices?
A: There are a few answers to this. You could insert scanned documents into your Word document, or you might need to save both your document and the appendix as PDFs and then join them together, using a tool like smallpdf If you need help, please contact nursing.edtech@keele.ac.uk
Remember: don't leave it until the last minute!

Q: How do I get my results & feedback?
A: On the day the results are released, go back to the dropbox where you submitted and there will be a View button to open your annotated document. The grade is shown in the top right.
